Privacy Policy

This page explains how Sorts collects, stores, and uses user information.

1. Introduction

Sorts is an AI-powered platform that allows users to connect and manage social media accounts and publish content across multiple providers.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Connected social media account information
  • OAuth access tokens
  • Scheduling preferences
  • Uploaded media (images, videos)
  • Usage analytics
  • Device and browser information

We never store user passwords for Google, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, X, or other providers.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide core features and improve reliability, including:

  • Connect social media accounts
  • Publish scheduled posts
  • Upload images and videos
  • Publish Instagram Reels
  • Publish YouTube videos
  • Improve platform reliability
  • Provide customer support
  • Secure user accounts

4. OAuth Authentication

Sorts uses official OAuth authentication provided by Google, YouTube, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), LinkedIn, and X (Twitter). Users may revoke permissions from the provider at any time.

5. Data Security

Tokens are encrypted where applicable, access is restricted, HTTPS is used, and industry-standard security practices are followed to protect data.

6. Data Sharing

Sorts does not sell personal data. Information is shared only with social providers when requested by the user or when required by law.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only while the account is active or until deletion is requested.

8. User Rights

Users may disconnect accounts, delete scheduled posts, request deletion of stored information, and revoke OAuth permissions directly from providers.

9. Contact

For questions or requests, contact us at support@sorts.app.
