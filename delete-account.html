Data Deletion Request

If you would like us to delete your data, follow the steps below. Deletion requests are processed by our support team.

Delete Your Data

You may request deletion of the following data associated with your Sorts account:

  • Connected social accounts
  • OAuth tokens
  • Uploaded media
  • Scheduled posts
  • Account profile
  • Analytics associated with the account

How to Request

  1. Email support@sorts.app
  2. Use subject "Delete My Account"
  3. Include the email associated with Sorts
  4. Requests are processed within 30 days

What Gets Deleted

✔ OAuth Tokens
✔ Scheduled Posts
✔ Uploaded Media
✔ Connected Accounts
✔ Account Information
✔ Analytics linked to the account

Important Note

Deleting Sorts data does not delete your Facebook, Instagram, Google, YouTube, LinkedIn, or X accounts. To remove Sorts access at the provider, revoke Sorts' permissions from the provider's account settings.
